# Sabbatical — Japan, 2027

A proper month off. Not "working remotely from Kyoto" — actually off.

## Itinerary shape

> The only bad version of this trip is one where I spent the whole time in Tokyo.

- **Week 1** — Tokyo. Get the jet lag out of the way. See friends who live there.
- **Week 2** — Kyoto + Nara. Slow pace, temples, walking.
- **Week 3** — Kumano Kodo (the pilgrimage hike). This is the whole reason.
- **Week 4** — Wildcard. Okinawa, or Hokkaido if it's summer. Pick closer to the date.

## Prerequisites

- Waypoint has to be in a state where I can be offline for a month. That means *at least* one other person on-call or an obvious graceful-degradation path.
- Enough runway in the bank that I'm not refreshing Stripe on the plane.

## Realistic trigger

If Waypoint hits the Q2 revenue targets, I can budget for this in Q4 2026 and book it for spring 2027.
