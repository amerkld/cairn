The current site is five years old and it shows. Not urgent — nobody's visiting it anyway — but if I'm going to be more public after Waypoint launches, it should reflect that.

## What I want

- Single-column, long-form. No sidebar, no header nav beyond a small `·` list.
- Serif for body text (*Source Serif*), mono for everything structural.
- Dark by default, respecting `prefers-color-scheme`.
- Every essay has a distinct opening — no templated header image.

## What I don't want

- ~~Fancy scroll animations~~
- ~~A "Now" page~~ (I'd never update it)
- ~~A newsletter signup in the middle of the essay~~
- ~~Tags~~ (I don't write enough for them to help)

## Platform

11ty or Astro. Whichever lets me write less config.
