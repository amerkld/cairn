The home studio (`Projects/Home-Studio/`) is the starter version. A dedicated detached shed is the real answer — 10'×14', its own electrical run, dust collection, a door wide enough to move a cabinet through.

## Non-negotiables

- Insulated walls (I want to work in January)
- Two separate 20A circuits
- At least one window on a non-adjacent wall for cross-ventilation
- Concrete floor, stained not sealed (I'll actually finish stain)

## Rough budget

~$15,000 all-in. Most of that is materials and a professional for the concrete pad and electrical.

## Trigger

The day I outgrow the garage. Track that by counting projects finished per quarter — if I'm hitting the layout limits more than twice a month, it's time.
