Two-tier plan. No free tier for v1 — too much cost exposure on map tiles.

| Plan          | Monthly | Annual          | Key limits                              |
| ------------- | ------- | --------------- | --------------------------------------- |
| **Explorer**  | $6      | $60 (save $12)  | 50 saved routes, offline on one device  |
| **Guide**     | $12     | $120 (save $24) | Unlimited routes, offline on 3 devices  |

## Copy direction

Lead with *what you get outdoors*, not feature lists:

> "Plan in the comfort of your couch. Navigate when the signal drops."

## Decisions made

- ~~Lifetime license tier~~ — attractive emotionally, terrible for LTV math. Skipping.
- ~~Free plan with one saved route~~ — abuse surface too big, see below.
- Annual discount at 2 months free. Round numbers are friendly.
