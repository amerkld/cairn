# J., 34, thru-hiker

Interviewed 2026-03-24 via Loom async + one 30-min follow-up call. ~6 months of beta usage.

## Context

Currently planning a continuous PCT attempt for Apr 2027. Uses Waypoint alongside Gaia and paper maps. Carries a Garmin inReach Mini.

## What's working

- Offline sync — "the only feature that actually matters to me"
- Route export to GPX for the Garmin
- The elevation profile "gets out of the way"

## What's not

- **Search is weak.** "If I save a route called 'Kennedy Meadows south,' I can't find it by typing 'kennedy.'" *— noted, this is a real bug.*
- No way to share a route with someone who hasn't installed the app. "If my friend is on iPhone and I'm on Android, I give up and text the trailhead name."

## Direct quote worth remembering

> "This is the first trail app that doesn't feel like it was designed in a conference room."

## Implications

1. Search normalization is low-hanging, ship before launch.
2. Public route links (`waypoint.app/r/<id>`) should be a day-one v1 feature. Renders a static map + route card, no install needed.
