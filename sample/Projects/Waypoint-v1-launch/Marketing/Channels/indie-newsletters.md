# Indie newsletters

Four outlets worth pitching. Warm outreach — no press releases, just short personal emails.

| Outlet           | Audience              | Contact      | Lead time needed | Status       |
| ---------------- | --------------------- | ------------ | ---------------- | ------------ |
| *Bootstrappers*  | ~18k indie founders   | Email reply  | 2 weeks          | Pitched Apr 14 |
| *Morning Dew*    | ~9k devs, eclectic    | Twitter DM   | 1 week           | Not yet      |
| *Trail Notes*    | ~12k hiking audience  | Web form     | 3 weeks          | Pitched Apr 8 |
| *Sidebar*        | ~40k design audience  | Email reply  | 1 week           | Not a fit    |

## Pitch template

> Hi [name] —
>
> Longtime reader. I'm launching a trail-planning app on May 11 called Waypoint. It's offline-first, designed by someone (me) who kept getting burned by online-only trail apps.
>
> Happy to write a guest post about the technical side if it's useful, or you can just link to the landing page — whatever fits your format. No pressure either way.
>
> — Sam

## Decisions

- **Skip** paid placements. Not in budget and not the right audience fit yet.
- **Skip** mass PR pitch lists. They're noise generators.
