# One-liner variations

Currently using: *"Waypoint is a trail-planning app that works when you don't have signal."*

Testing alternatives against the two segments surfaced in `../Research/findings.md`.

| # | Variation                                                                   | For segment          |
| - | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------- |
| 1 | A trail-planning app that works when you don't have signal.                 | Both *(baseline)*    |
| 2 | Plan the hike on the couch. Navigate it in the canyon.                      | Weekenders           |
| 3 | The map that still works at mile 180.                                       | Thru-hikers          |
| 4 | Offline-first trail maps that don't feel like a GIS tool.                   | Weekenders           |
| 5 | ~~The hiker's companion.~~ *too generic, kills on specificity*              | —                    |

## Decision

**Variation 1** for the landing page hero. Keeps it honest and works for both segments. Variations 2 and 3 can live as secondary copy on feature pages.

## Next step

Run #1, #2, #3 through a 10-person reaction test this week. Look for *"which would make you click?"* and note *hesitations*, not just enthusiasm.
