Goal: get a new user to their *first saved offline route* within 48 hours of signup.

| # | Sent         | Subject                                       | One-sentence goal                   |
| - | ------------ | --------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------- |
| 1 | T+0          | "Welcome — here's how I'd start"              | Plant the "offline-first" mental model. |
| 2 | T+24h        | "Save a route, then step away from wifi"      | Push them to complete the key step. |
| 3 | T+72h        | "Three routes the beta crew saved this week"  | Social proof + the discovery hook.  |

Write in the same voice as the app copy — plain, specific, no hype.
