Tweet thread + Show HN + blog post. Same beats, different lengths.

**Beats:**

1. Hook — "the map app that works when there's no signal"
2. Why I built it — three near-misses on trail, one of them mine
3. What it does — 90 seconds of screenshots/video
4. What's next — iOS, Apple Watch, import from Strava
5. Call to action — `https://waypoint.test`

Keep the tone **direct and honest**. No hype language. No emoji chains. Dan reviews the final draft.
