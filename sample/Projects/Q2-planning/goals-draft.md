# Q2 2026 — goals

Three pillars. Each pillar gets one headline goal and up to three supporting outcomes.

## 1. Launch and grow

> Waypoint goes public, and enough people use it that the next quarter is about improving, not convincing.

- Ship v1 by **May 15**
- 500 paid signups by **end of June**
- NPS ≥ 40 from paying users by **end of June**

## 2. Financial runway

> Get the business to break-even or a clear path to it, so Q3 planning isn't dominated by money anxiety.

- Monthly recurring revenue ≥ $3,000 by end of quarter
- One enterprise conversation started (doesn't need to close)
- Burn rate documented and stable

## 3. Me

> Don't burn out shipping v1.

- Two full weekends off per month, non-negotiable
- One week fully unplugged in June
- Resume the Tuesday / Thursday gym cadence
